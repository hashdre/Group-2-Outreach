Welcome to our project, a collaborative effort by Group 2 of 9 - St. Luke. We are dedicated to supporting the orphans of the Meritxell community residing at 24 Peach St. Marikina. Our program goes beyond simple donations, focusing on sustainable assistance through a series of planned activities.
