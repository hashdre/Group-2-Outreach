About the Project

Our project, "Mga Kamay ng Pag-asa: Gabay sa Landas ng mga Ulila" is an outreach initiative by Group 2 aimed at supporting the Meritxell Children’s Home in Marikina City, a foundation established in 2008 to provide a safe, loving environment for abandoned, orphaned, and abused girls aged 0-15. Driven by compassion and a shared sense of purpose, we’ve designed activities focused on fun, education, and emotional well-being. These include interactive games and discussions centered on Good Manners and Right Conduct (GMRC), fostering values that help the children navigate daily life. The project also includes distributing much-needed supplies like clothing, school materials, and toys, collected through donations and class contributions. By ensuring sustainability, such as consistent community engagement and fostering emotional growth, we aim to make a lasting impact, instilling hope and positivity in the lives of these children. Our efforts are a collective testament to empathy, teamwork, and the transformative power of outreach.
